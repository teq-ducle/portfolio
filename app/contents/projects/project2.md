## Project Introduction:

[Vuong Khang Home](https://vuongkhanghome.com/) is a website dedicated to showcasing **home design and construction services**, including building, interior renovation, and providing comprehensive solutions for modern living spaces.

This project was developed to help potential customers easily access information about construction services, explore completed projects, and connect with professional consultants.

**Main objectives of the project:**

- Create a professional and visually appealing interface that reflects the high-quality services offered by Vương Khang Home.
- Improve website SEO and performance using **NuxtJS** for better search engine visibility and faster loading speeds.
- Enhance user experience with an intuitive and well-structured layout, making navigation seamless and efficient.

![Project 1 Interface](/projects/project1/vuongkhanghome.webp)

## Key Features:

- Fully responsive design that adapts to all screen sizes, from desktops to mobile devices.
- A well-structured service catalog covering: **design, construction, and interior renovation**.
- A portfolio showcasing completed projects, giving potential clients a clear view of previous work.
- An interactive contact form that allows users to request consultations effortlessly.
- Optimized images and caching techniques for a faster browsing experience.

## Technologies Used:

- **NuxtJS**: Enables server-side rendering for better SEO and performance, ensuring a smooth and dynamic user experience.
- **SCSS**: Provides a structured and scalable approach to styling, making it easier to maintain and expand the design.
- **Lazy Loading & Caching**: Improves site performance by loading images and resources only when needed.

## Achievements:

- Successfully delivered a stable and high-performance website with fast loading times.
- Improved SEO rankings, increasing online visibility and attracting more potential clients.
- A modern and professional website design that enhances brand credibility and user engagement.
- Enhanced mobile compatibility, ensuring an excellent experience across all devices.
